## What's here

**[Writing](/writing/)** — the blog. Posts live in `_posts/` as `YYYY-MM-DD-slug.md`.

**[Wiki](/wiki/)** — a digital garden. Pages live in `_wiki/<folder>/<page>.md` and
index themselves by folder. Stock, not flow.

**[Library](/library/)** — things worth keeping, with fuzzy search over titles, tags
and links.

**[Links](/links/)** — a stream of quotes and things worth reading.

**[Graphics](/graphics/)** — blinkies, 88x31 buttons, stickers, pixels and other
web ephemera. Take what you like.

**[Feeds](/feeds/)** — the blogs I read, rendered from an OPML file you can
[grab for yourself](/feeds.opml).
